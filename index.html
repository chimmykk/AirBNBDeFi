Our Key Features

AirBnb DeFi combines the remarkable service of AirBnb and power of DeFi to offer a unique feature for both individual and professional home owners in Cryptocurrency.

Fully Decentralized

With privacy and security being a crucial aspect to life, we provide the best decentralized home booking service platform for everyone seeking a safe, secure and private abode.

Easy and Reliable

We are providing a user friendly platform for users to seamlessly make or receive payment with $ABD.

Security and Convenience

AirBnb Defi ensures users booking and hosting through the platform maintain the highest security standards. This also applies to token vesting, locked liquidity all of which are fully compliant.
